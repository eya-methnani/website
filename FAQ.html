FAQs

What is the Positive and Sociable Citizens club? +

The Positive and Sociable Citizens club is a student-run club founded in 2011 within the National School of Computer Science ENSI in Tunisia. The club's main objective is to promote social awareness, civic responsibility, and leadership among its members.

Who can join the Positive and Sociable Citizens club? +

Membership of the Positive and Sociable Citizens club is open to all students at the National School of Computer Science ENSI in Tunisia, regardless of their field of study or background.

What are the benefits of joining the Positive and Sociable Citizens club? +

Benefits of joining the Positive and Sociable Citizens club include opportunities to develop leadership and interpersonal skills, participate in community service activities, network with other students and professionals, and make a positive impact on society.

How can I get involved with the Positive and Sociable Citizens club? +

To get involved with the Positive and Sociable Citizens club, you can attend one of their events or meetings, reach out to the club directly to find out more about membership, or participate in one of their community service projects or initiatives.

How can I contact the Positive and Sociable Citizens club for more information or to join? +

You can contact the Positive and Sociable Citizens club by visiting their website, emailing them directly, or by attending one of their events or meetings to find out more about membership and other opportunities to get involved.

The camping events are a fun and meaningful way to raise funds and awareness for a cause while providing an opportunity for participants to enjoy the outdoors and bond with each other. The event typically involves a group of volunteers, donors, or supporters of the charity camping together in a designated area, such as a national park or private campsite.Overall, a charity camping event can be a great way to promote community involvement, raise awareness, and support a meaningful cause while enjoying the great outdoors and building connections with like-minded individuals.

School visit

A school visit by CPS is an educational and inspiring event where members visit a school to educate students about their cause, work, and impact. The visit can take various forms, such as presentations, workshops, interactive sessions, or hands-on activities, depending on the age group and interests of the students. The purpose of the visit is to raise awareness about the issues the charity addresses, encourage empathy and social responsibility, and inspire students to get involved in volunteering or fundraising efforts. Such visits can also be a great opportunity for schools to build community partnerships and support local organizations.

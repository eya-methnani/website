WHO ARE WE ?

Positive and Sociable Citizens is a club founded in 2011 within the national school of computer science ENSI, supported by the regional committee of the Tunisian Red Crescent in La Manouba, it is in cooperation with worthy associations and organizations trust of civil society. In its beginnings, it had a purely awareness-raising and ambulatory health purpose. Now, our club is no longer limited to these two axes, a humanitarian and voluntary axis is added to all its activities. Every year, Citoyens Positifs et Sociables organizes first aid training, blood donation days, visits to child protection centers, visits to centers for the protection of the elderly, and finally visits to primary schools in the rural areas. The club's visits to schools are very well studied and are based on several criteria: standard of living of the areas to be visited, climatic conditions, financial conditions.

Some of our most prominent charitable works:

  • Elementary school help
  • Assistance in the vaccination campaign against the Corona virus
  • helping the poor with food and clothing
  • camping
